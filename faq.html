About Neural Music Group (NMG)

NMG is an AI-powered record label, publishing company, and creative ecosystem designed for the next generation of artists. We merge human creativity, artificial intelligence, holistic wellness principles, modern marketing systems, and ethical business structure to help artists build sustainable, ownership-driven careers.

NMG was founded by Anthony Boyd, drawing from over three decades of experience in music production, songwriting, audio engineering, publishing, design and branding, holistic health and wellness, creative systems development, and AI innovation.

After years of seeing independent artists struggle with chaos, predatory contracts, and outdated industry models, it became clear that artists didn't need another traditional label‚Äîthey needed an ecosystem. NMG was built as that solution: not a label you sign your life away to, but a label you build with.

The mission of NMG is to empower modern artists with:

  • Ownership and creative sovereignty
  • AI-enhanced creation and production support
  • Transparent, fair publishing structures
  • Holistic support for mental, emotional, and creative wellness
  • Modern marketing and release systems
  • Long-term partnership and leadership pathways

We help artists not just release songs, but build catalogs, brands, incomes, and legacies.

NMG's vision is to become the world's most ethical, AI-powered, creator-focused label‚Äîwhere artists grow in their music, their identity, and their leadership.

NMG functions as:

Publishing, Rights & Legal

NMG uses fair, transparent publishing structures. We encourage:

  • Clear split sheets for every song
  • Agreed percentages for all songwriters and publishers
  • Written agreements before release
  • Educating artists on basic publishing and royalty concepts

We are not here to confuse you‚Äîwe are here to help you understand how your money and rights work.

No. NMG is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice. We can:
