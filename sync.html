Sync Workflow
Whether you're submitting music or licensing it, the path is straightforward. No mystery, no runaround.
Transparent + Traceable
Step 01
Submit & Tag

Creators share 2–5 of their strongest tracks with basic metadata, splits, and streaming/download links via our submission form.

Step 02
Review & Fit

We review for quality, sonic fit, and clearance complexity, then decide whether to onboard into the NMG Publishing + Sync catalog.

Step 03
Onboard & Clear

If accepted, we lock in splits, confirm PROs, request alt mixes where needed, and add the songs to our internal sync universe.

Step 04
Pitch, Place, Pay

We pitch against briefs, respond to supervisor requests, close licenses, and ensure creator payouts are tracked and reported.
